# Gymble Branding Guidelines

## Core Identity

**Gymble** is the operating system for sports businesses, helping venues and trainers streamline their operations, bookings, and growth.

### Mission

To empower sports businesses with a modern, all-in-one platform that handles the complexity of management so they can focus on their passion.

## Visual Identity

- **Aesthetic**: Modern, Clean, Professional, Trustworthy.
- **Core Color**: **Electric Blue (`#0b5cff`)** represents energy and technology.
- **Style**: High usage of white space, rounded corners (8px for buttons, full for pills), and soft shadows.

## Tone of Voice

- **Professional**: We are a business tool, so we speak with clarity and competence.
- **Empowering**: We help our users succeed.
- **Direct**: We don't use jargon; we get straight to the point.

## Typography

- **Primary Font**: `DM Sans`
- **Fallback**: system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif
- **Weights**:
  - Headings: Bold (700)
  - Body: Regular (400) / Medium (500)

## Logo Usage

- **Mark**: The Gymble "G" or logomark.
- **Wordmark**: Clean, sans-serif typography.
- **Clearspace**: Maintain generous padding around the logo.
